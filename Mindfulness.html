Everyday Mindfulness Practices

Start with the breath

Take a few slow, deep breaths when you wake up, before meals, or when stressed.

Notice the air moving in and out without trying to change it.

Mindful moments

Choose small daily activities (brushing teeth, drinking tea, washing hands) and do them slowly with full attention.

Notice the sounds, textures, tastes, and sensations.

Body scan

Close your eyes and slowly move your attention from head to toe.

Notice areas of tension without judgment—just observe and gently relax.

Mindful walking

Walk slowly, paying attention to how your feet touch the ground.

Notice your surroundings instead of rushing or thinking ahead.

Gratitude pause

Each night, name one or two things you’re grateful for.

This trains your mind to notice positives instead of only stress.
