General Nutrition & Wellness Tips


Eat a Balanced Plate

Try to include protein, healthy fats, complex carbs, and vegetables in every meal.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 7–8 glasses of water daily. Sometimes thirst feels like hunger.

Move Daily

Even light exercise, like walking or stretching, supports calorie balance and overall wellness.

Rest & Manage Stress

Good sleep and stress management are as important as diet for overall health.
